Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026 — draft, pending attorney review
The short version: we use one strictly-necessary cookie to keep you signed in. We don't use advertising or tracking cookies, and we don't sell data to anyone.

What This Covers

This policy describes the cookies used by the Sorted & Stocked website and application. It's referenced from our Privacy Policy.

Cookies We Use

TypePurposeConsent required?
Authentication (Supabase)Keeps you signed in between visits so you don't have to log in repeatedly.No — strictly necessary for the service to function.

That's the complete list as of this writing. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or cross-site tracking of any kind.

Analytics

We may add privacy-focused analytics in the future (such as Plausible) to understand how the site is used. Plausible-style analytics tools operate without cookies and without tracking individual visitors, so this would not add anything to the table above. If that changes, this policy will be updated first.

Your Choices

Because our only cookie is strictly necessary for signing in, there isn't a marketing or tracking cookie to opt out of. If you'd prefer not to use the authentication cookie, the practical effect is that you won't be able to stay signed in to the app.

Changes to This Policy

If our cookie usage changes — for example, if we add analytics or any third-party service that sets cookies — we'll update this page and reflect the change in our Privacy Policy.
